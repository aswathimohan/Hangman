TORNADO =  A violently rotating column of air that is in contact with both the surface of the earth and a cumulonimbus cloud

WILDFIRE = An uncontrolled fire in an area of combustible vegetation that occurs in the countryside area

CARBON DIOXIDE = A colorless, odorless gas produced by burning carbon and organic compounds and by respiration

FLOOD = An overflow of water that submerges land which is usually dry

DROUGHT = A period of below-average precipitation in a given region, resulting in prolonged shortages in its water supply, whether atmospheric, surface or groundwater
